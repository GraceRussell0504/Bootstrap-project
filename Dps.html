What is the job of DPS?

DPS play one main role: deal damage. Depending on which DPS is playing, their job is either to break the enemy tanks shield, or target the enemy's supports so their team can't be healed. A DPS' job is also to help protect their support against poking characters.


Meet three of our DPS

  • Widowmaker
      • Widow's main weapon is her sniper rifle. It has no damage drop-off and will one-shot and enemy support or dps with one headshot.
  • Tracer
      • Tracer is a close-range character he does very little damage, but she can pick off enemy supports who are unaware of their surroundings easily.
  • Soldier
      • Soldier has a regular rifle that does high-damage but it has a good amount of recoil. He also has rockets that he can launch every few seconds that are hard to hit, but do a lot of damage.
