What is the job of Support?

Support's main job is to heal the team and keep them alive during combat. Also, every support has a very powerful and unique utility for the team, such as speed boost, revive, healing buffers, and making the max health of an ally higher. There are several supports who also can do poking damage here and there.


Meet three of our support heroes

  • Mercy
      • She has two main abilities, healing beam and damage boost. On top of that she has the ability to fly to her teammates from long distances and revive them.
  • Ana
      • Ana wields a nanobiotic gun and grenade. These both will heal teammates and damage enemies. Ana also wields a sleep dart that can be used to protect herself or her team.
  • Zenyatta
      • Zen doesn't heal a lot, but he deals a lot of damage. Zenyatta wields a "discord orb" that will tag one target on the enemy team and make them take 30% more damage.
