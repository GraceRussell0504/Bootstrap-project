What is the role of a tank?

A tanks job is to protect the front-line and push the team forward. Tanks can either target the backline of the enemy team or stay back and protect their own team. Tanks have a very large health pool and deal high damage, but are reliant on their supports to keep them alive.


Meet three of our tanks

  • Reinhardt
      • Rein has a very large health pool of 650. He wields a shield that has 1200 health and will recharge over time. He also has a hammer that deals 85 damage per swing.
  • D.va
      • Dva has a health pool of 600 and is a close-range combat tank. She has two shotguns that have infinite ammo as well as a matrix that descintegrates all projectiles and lasts for three seconds.
  • Roadhog
      • Rodhog has 700 health and has a self-healing ability that heals 350 HP. He also has a hook and shotgun combo that can one-shot any DPS or Support on the enemy team.
